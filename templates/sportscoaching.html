Sports Coaching & Development

This placement is open to volunteers interested in providing skills coaching and organising matches, tournaments, etc. for local teams during and after school hours.

Many children between 12 and 18 years are part of local teams in schools, and also participate in sporting activities outside of school settings. The types of sporting activities include football, volleyball, netball, rugby and so on.
