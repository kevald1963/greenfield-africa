Building & Refurbishment

Our building placement project consist of new builds, refurbishments and extensions to existing buildings. The types of buildings we work on are schools, clinics, orphanages, toilets etc., usually in villages where there is little or no provision from governmental authorities. We have recently (November 2023) completed a teachers’ accommodation block to cater for 3 teachers in a remote village of Ghana called Iddrisu-Krom. In addition to the teacher’s accommodation, we had in 2021/2022 extended and refurbished the village school building with a new classroom and provided furniture and educational materials.

In Ghana, Kenya and Malawi, many families cannot afford to pay for their children’s school fees, so the latter often end up with few skills or qualifications, and are invariably condemned to a life of menial work and poverty. Greenfield’s objective is to break this cycle of poverty. You can be part of that by contributing to our building projects either through physical volunteer participation or through donation. The main work in the villages where building projects are executed is mostly farming. If you are interested in seeing how farming is carried out in rural parts of Ghana, Kenya or Malawi, a visit to one or more farms can be arranged for you during your project.
