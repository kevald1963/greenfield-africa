How to apply

Applying is easy. Just email kevin_alderson@yahoo.co.uk with your chosen destination, type of volunteering activity (or activities, if you wish to more than one), the approximate departure date, and the duration of stay.

On receipt of your email we will confirm if your choices are available, or suggest possible alternatives if your choices are not available. There is no obligation at this stage. We just want to be sure we can accommodate your choices before proceeding any further.

You can also use the above email address to make any enquiries about volunteering with us, or about Greenfield in general.
