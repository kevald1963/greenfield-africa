Project Ghana

Greenfield Africa was founded in Ghana in 2013 by Gideon Korda. After many years of working with a UK-based NGO, Gideon decided to establish Greenfield Africa Foundation with the sole aim of contributing to community development especially in favour of disadvantaged communities which had been neglected by the authorities.

In Ghana, Greenfield undertakes the following projects: Teaching & Childcare placement, Medical & Healthcare placement, Building placement and Sport and Coaching placement. These projects are all open to both young individuals and any category of persons interested to physically partake in any of our projects. Greenfield's projects are all flexible for alternative arrangements.
