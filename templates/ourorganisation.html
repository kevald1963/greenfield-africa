Our Organisation

Thank you for considering Greenfield Africa. The information provided here is intended to give you a general background about our organisation.

Greenfield Africa is a not-for-profit NGO that is committed to alleviating poverty and disadvantage in various African countries. Currently, we operate in 3 different countries: Ghana, Kenya and Malawi.

Although we always refer publicly to the organisation as Greenfield Africa, it is worth noting that, internally there are currently two parts to Greenfield Africa: Greenfield Africa Foundation and Greenfield Africa UK.

Greenfield Africa Foundation was originally established in 2013 by a Ghanaian, Gideon Korda, under the laws of Ghana. Greenfield Africa Foundation has two arms of operations: administrative and executive functions to ensure the success of all projects undertaken in every project destination of the organisation. It has over the years maintained contractual relationship with competent, skillful, committed and honest trained individuals in the execution of the objectives of the Foundation's projects across all destinations. Gideon is the Project Director for all 3 project destinations (Ghana, Kenya and Malawi). He is committed to ensuring the success of all our projects.

Facebook page: https://www.facebook.com/GreenfieldAfrica.Gh

Greenfield Africa UK (commonly referred to as Greenfield UK, for short) is the UK branch for Greenfield Africa. This branch was established in 2021 through the hard work and support of Kevin Alderson. Its core purpose is to support Greenfield Africa Foundation in fundraising activities and the recruitment of UK & international volunteers to projects in Ghana, Kenya and Malawi. It ensures funds dedicated towards projects in all project destinations are spent for the sole purpose for which they are intended. It also ensures the welfare and concerns of visiting volunteers are not compromised in any form. Kevin is the Country Director of Greenfield Africa UK. He manages and co-ordinates the affairs of the charity through the support of few committed International citizens namely: Hannah Price (New Zealand), Becky Price (UK) and Riccardo Giordano (Italy). Greenfield UK is a charity and has its own board of trustees, namely: Robert Bonati (Chair), Becky Price (Secretary), and Kevin Alderson (Treasurer). The board is assisted with publicity and fundraising by Officers Bella Ali and Anne Patterson. The charity is currently seeking recognition with HMRC to enhance its project donations through Gift Aid.
