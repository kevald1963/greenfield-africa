Greenfield Development Funding

Apart from running volunteering programmes, Greenfield Foundation provides small grants to various projects in Ghana and also to our partner NGO in Kenya, Greencard Mtaani. In time, we hope to extend grants to projects in Malawi. We are currently in negotiations with an NGO in that country.

Follow the links below to see what we have funded in Ghana and Kenya so far.

GHANA PROJECTS

  • Building of Day Care Centre at Peki Tsame Primary School, Volta Region.

KENYA PROJECTS

  • Refurbishment Greencard Mtaaani's education centre, Kibera, Nairobi.
