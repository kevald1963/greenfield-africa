Greenfield Development Funding

Apart from running volunteering programmes, Greenfield Foundation also provides small grants to various projects in Ghana and Kenya. In Ghana, we fund projects selected by our own government-registered NGO, Greenfield Foundation, whereas in Kenya we currently work with a partner NGO called Greencard Mtaani. In time, we hope to extend our development funding programme to projects in Malawi. We are currently negotiating a partnership agreement with an NGO there.

All our prospective, and existing, NGO partners must be registered with the relevant authorities in their country, in order for Greenfield to work with them.

The links below show what Greenfield has funded in Ghana and Kenya so far.
