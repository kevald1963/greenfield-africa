Repairs after flooding at Greencard Mtaani, Kenya

In early 2024, Greencard Mtaani's Mailaka Education Centre, in Kibera, Nairobi, was flooded as a result of heavy rains causing the nearby river to overflow. In response to Greencard's appeal, Greenfield provided funding, in June 2024, to help it buy materials to repair and refurbish the classrooms that were damaged. Some of the funding was also used to build stronger defences around the classrooms to help stop this happening again. The large stones, shown below, will form part of these defences.
