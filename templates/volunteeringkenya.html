Project Kenya

In Kenya, Greenfield is in partnership with two inner-city NGOs in the district of Kibra, a slum district of Nairobi. Most, but not all, volunteering is based in Malaika School, which is run by our partner NGO, Greencard Mtaani. What we offer here is a mix of activities, that all volunteers are expected to assist with. The main volunteering activities are building & refurbishment, teaching of basic Maths & English, and assisting with Sports / P.E. lessons, but you will be also asked to assist with general tasks needed to run the school such as assisting with cooking, serving food and clearing up.

For those interested in Medical & Healthcare volunteering, we offer a placement at Langata Sub-County Hospital.

If you decide to volunteer with us in Kenya, you need to book your outbound flight to Nairobi's main airport, Jomo Kenyatta International Airport (IATA code: NBO). Flights to Kenya are operated by a number of carriers from the UK, including KLM, Air France, British Airways and Kenya Airways. Most flights from Newcastle, and other major UK provincial cities, include a transfer via a European hub such as Amsterdam, Paris, Frankfurt, etc. Some direct flights are available from London.
